Wonder Studio

Book salon services in seconds. Login with Firebase, manage bookings, and cancel anytime. Built with Flutter — looks real, works like one.

What Is It?

Wonder Studio is a polished, real-app-feeling beauty salon booking interface built for Flutter (Dart) and designed for Figma mockups. The experience begins with two animated splash screens featuring the brand logo fading in over soft, elegant imagery. Users then log in or sign up via Firebase — with email fields and a “Sign Up with Google” option — before landing on a welcoming home screen that highlights top artists and customer reviews. From there, users tap into “Services” to browse offerings like Hair Dye, Makeup, and Facial with clear pricing in PKR. Tapping “Book a Service” opens a simple, step-by-step form: select service, pick date/time, add special requests, then confirm. The bottom navigation bar (Home / Services / Bookings / Profile) ensures seamless flow, and while not shown, the “Bookings” tab should include a delete/cancel feature — visually represented by a trash icon next to each appointment to solve the problem of managing unwanted bookings. Every screen is clean, consistent, and ready to be turned into a working prototype or live app.
