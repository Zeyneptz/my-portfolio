Food Court

Order your favorite meals in seconds. Login with Firebase, manage your orders, and delete what you don’t want — all in one sleek app.

What Is It?

Food Court is a smart, user-friendly food ordering app built for speed, simplicity, and control. Log in securely with Firebase Authentication to personalize your experience — save your favorites, track past orders, and manage your cart with ease. Browse mouth-watering dishes like Dal Tadka or Chicken Kharyai, add them to your cart with one tap, and checkout instantly. View your active orders in real-time and delete or modify them before confirmation — no more accidental orders! Designed for real-world use, every screen — from login to profile to order history — feels intuitive and polished
